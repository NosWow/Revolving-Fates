# REVOLVING FATES

In an era where empires clash and destinies intertwine, four young men—a philosopher seeking enlightenment, an exiled prince with vengeance in his heart, and two warrior brothers scarred by the past—come together by chance and by choice.

Bound by a blood oath, they embark on a quest to change a world torn by tyranny and conflict.

As they rise to power in different lands, alliances will be forged and betrayals will test their resolve. A relentless rebel leader threatens them all, and the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

In a world where loyalty is tested and ambition has a price, how far will they go to shape their own fates?

## Prologue

A wind blew sand across the town of Konyle. It was a peaceful settlement in the south-west of Dun'kara, the capital of a country named Kabara. Tribes lived there.

Women were weaving nets and pieces of cloth. Men were earning the dough for their houses. Children happily played along the streets. It was a peaceful time.

Yet an ominous shadow loomed over the calm atmosphere.

Dun'kara was ruled by a capitalist power named Caelshia, hailing from the north. Their acts were brutal, barbaric, and showed no mercy. The tribes yearned to quench their thirst for freedom.

Soon, darkness fell upon the sky.

The Caelshians were patrolling the streets. Children were running about, playing tag after a hard day at school. One small girl, Nayomara, was among them. She tagged a boy and started running in a direction she wasn't aware of.

**Bump!**

Nayomara ran into a lanky, cranky Caelshian soldier.

She gave him a smile that could melt anyone.

"Sorry, sir!" she said sweetly, raising a small salute.

The soldier snarled.

**Bang.**

That was the last day anyone ever saw those kids.
